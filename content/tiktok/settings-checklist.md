# TikTok settings checklist

## Account safety and moderation
- Enable 2FA
- Set comment filters for spam and offensive language
- Enable keyword filters
- Restrict duets/stitches for risky posts if needed
- Keep DM policy clear in bio and comments

## Suggested filtered words
- spam terms: crypto, giveaway, free money, invest now
- hate speech and insults in Romanian and English
- explicit sexual terms
- harassment terms targeting religion or users

## Content and posting settings
- Turn on analytics for creator account
- Set timezone correctly
- Keep auto captions on for accessibility
- Use cover text on every video

## Community policy guardrails
- No fake urgency
- No guilt-based pressure for donations
- No religious absolutist claims on behalf of God
- Always add gentle disclaimers when discussing sensitive topics
